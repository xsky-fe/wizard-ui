将 Panel 用在元素上。

## 何时使用

- 增强文案显示

### 常规

```jsx
<div>
  <Panel>i am panel </Panel>
</div>
```

### 带颜色

```jsx
<div>
  <Panel bsStyle="warning">i am panel </Panel>
  <Panel bg="primary" text="white">
    i am panel and have primary bg color{' '}
  </Panel>
  <Panel text="danger">i am panel and have danger text color </Panel>
</div>
```

### 带头部或底部

```jsx
<div>
  <Panel header={<p>i am panel header</p>}>
    i am panel and have danger text color{' '}
  </Panel>
  <Panel footer={<p>i am panel footer</p>}>
    i am panel and have danger text color{' '}
  </Panel>
</div>
```
